Inter-Panel Relationship Summary

Composition Relationships (♦):
• AttendanceDashboardPanel ♦→ AttendanceMarkingPanel, AttendanceHistoryPanel
• Grade11EnhancedDashboard ♦→ Grade11SelfAttendancePanel
• ClubAllocationPanel ♦→ ClubMembersPanel, ClubInfoPanel
• ManagerClubAssignmentsViewPanel ♦→ ManagerAttendanceReportPanel

Aggregation Relationships (◊):
• AttendanceMarkingPanel ◊→ AttendanceHistoryPanel (data sharing)
• Grade11SelfAttendancePanel ◊→ Grade9SelfAttendancePanel (attendance data)
• ClubMembersPanel ◊→ ClubInfoPanel (club data sharing)
• All Manager panels ◊→ Regular panels (data consumption)

Inheritance Relationships (▲):
• All panels ▲→ JPanel (framework inheritance)
